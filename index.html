Professional Experience

Summer 2018:

  • Integritas Analytics

    Integritas Analytics is a data science consulting company based out of Lake Forest, Illinois. Our main project for the summer was building a comprehensive market research deck for Greenwhich Biosciences conerning their FDA-approved CBD drug: Epidiolex.

  • Joseki Technologies

    Joseki Tech is a web development company from Pennsylvania. We worked together to build efficient, scalable React.js Web Apps for high-profile clients. Throughout the experience, I've learned lots about the ins and outs of professional web-dev and computer science.

  • Independent Work

    In my off time from the aforementioned two jobs, I work on my own independent projects. One example is my undergraduate thesis, though I'll be starting senior year with plenty of work ahead. The application will be leverageing computer vision and machine learning to reconstruct a solve of the Rubick's Cube, which can be blisteringly fast.

Pre-2018:

  • University of Florida, Summer 2017

    Under Professor Corey Toler-Franklin, I conducted computer graphics research as a staff member in the CISE department. My work was to be used as a step in the pipeline for an augmented-reality application, published at SIGGRAPH. I grew as a professional computer scientist and researcher, and couldn't be more thankful to Dr. Toler-Franklin for the experience.

  • Thrive+ LLC., Summer 2016

    Thrive+ is a consumer packaged goods startup. We received $20,000 in seed funding from Princeton's eLab startup accelerator program, as well as fully covered on-campus housing for the summer. Working in a small startup, I contributed everything from market research and business intelligence, to front end and back end web development.
